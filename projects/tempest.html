Introduction

Made as part of the final project in the Deep Learning course I had at ITU in the autumn of 2022. We made an AI agent that could play a series of scenarios in ViZDoom. VizDoom allows for training AI agents to play the original Doom, you can read more here: https://vizdoom.cs.put.edu.pl/. The aim of the project was for us to compare different kinds of Deep Reinforcement Learning algorithms and compare their performance.

Each planet has the same type of atmosphere. The atmosphere effect is calculated from the Rayleigh scattering reflecting more blue light back at the camera. Rayleigh scattering is the scattering of light by small particles in the atmosphere. It causes shorter wavelengths blue to scatter more than longer wavelengths, which is why the sky is blue. As the amount of blue wavelength light is reduced, the more reddish the light reflected becomes as the light travels through the atmosphere, the redder the reflected light gets. This is why sunsets are reddish.
